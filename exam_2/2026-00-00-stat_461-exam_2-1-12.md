## Problem 12

The newest multi-stage rocket that NASA is planning to use for its upcoming James Webb space telescope consists of 3 stages. As soon as one dies (i.e. completely burns up all its fuel), the next stage immediately takes over. If the lifespan of each stage (i.e. the time it takes to burn up all its fuel) has an Exponential distribution with mean 6 minutes and if the 3 lifespans are independent of one another, what are the mean and the variance of the rocket's total lifespan? What are the chances that even the smallest of the three individual life spans is > 5 minutes?
