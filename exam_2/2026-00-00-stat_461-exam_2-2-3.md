## Problem 3

You can reach your office from home either directly via I-77 or by first taking I-271 north and then taking SR-8 south. There's no other way of reaching your office. On a usual weekday morning, the chances of I-77 being closed due to an accident is 0.1, that of I-271 north being closed due to an accident is 0.08 and that of SR-8 south being closed is 0.05. Assuming that the traffic conditions on these 3 highways are independent of one another, what are your chances of reaching the office on a typical weekday morning? Suppose the traffic condition on any particular day is independent of that on any other day. What are the chances that, in the next 3 days, you will fail to reach your office twice due to accident-related traffic congestion?
