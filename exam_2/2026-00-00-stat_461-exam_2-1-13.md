## Problem 13

At a toll booth on an interstate highway, the inter-arrival time between two cars (i.e. the time between the arrival of one car and the next) during the off-peak hours of 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM has an Exponential distribution with mean 8 minutes. If the person manning the toll booth this evening saw a car at 8:30 PM and then nothing till 8:45 PM, what is the conditional probability that he/she won't see a car till 9:00?
