# Problem 4

Durations of 911 phone calls are known to have a population distribution that is skewed to the right, with a population mean of 12 minutes and a population standard deviation of 3.2 minutes. If you take a simple random sample of 64 such phone calls and record their durations, what will be the approximate distribution of the sample average? What are the chances that the sample average will:

(a) exceed 13 minutes?

(b) be below 10.8 minutes?

Hint: Use the Central Limit Theorem.
