# Problem 20

20 patients with severe epilepsy were observed for 8 weeks for seizures. The following are the numbers of major seizures suffered by each patient during that period:

$$
5,0,9,6,0,0,5,0,6,1,5,0,0,0,0,7,0,0,4,7.
$$

(a) What is the mean, or average, number of seizures?

(b) What is the five-point summary of this dataset?

(c) Draw a modified boxplot with the fences clearly marked and do outlier detection.
