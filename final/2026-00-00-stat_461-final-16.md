# Problem 16

A bowl has 6 red marbles and 2 blue marbles in it. Another bowl has 2 red marbles and 2 blue marbles. Reaching into the first bowl and without looking, you pick a marble and transfer it to the second bowl. Now, if you reach into the second bowl to grab a marble without looking and the marble turns out to be red, what are the conditional chances that the marble transferred earlier was red too?
