# Problem 10

If you have a simple random sample $X_1,\ldots,X_n$ from a Weibull$(3,\beta)$ population, derive the maximum likelihood estimate of $\beta$.

Hint: First write down the sample likelihood function. Then write down the natural log of the sample likelihood function. Then differentiate it with respect to $\beta$ and equate it to 0 to get the solution. Lastly, check if the second derivative with respect to $\beta$ is less than 0.
