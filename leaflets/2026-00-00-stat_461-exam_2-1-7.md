## Problem 7

Three players (Tom, Dick and Harry) are playing the following game: Starting with Tom, they take turns (in that particular order) to roll a special die that has '3' marked on three of its sides, '2' marked on two of its sides and '1' on the remaining side. Whoever gets a '1' for the first time wins the game. Find $P(\text{Tom wins})$, $P(\text{Dick wins})$ and $P(\text{Harry wins})$. If it is given that Harry has won, what are the conditional chances that he won on his 1st attempt?
