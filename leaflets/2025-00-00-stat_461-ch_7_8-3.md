## Problem 3

A simple random sample of 16 pygmy goats from Cameroon (a West African country) that were exported to European zoos had a sample average height of 51 centimeters and a sample standard deviation of heights = 3.2 centimeters. If heights of these animals are known to follow a Normal curve, what is a 98% confidence interval for the true, unknown population mean height of that species in the entire country? What is a 95% confidence interval for the true, unknown population variance of their heights?
