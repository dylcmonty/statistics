## Problem 1

In order to see if a new brand of dietary iron supplement can have a significant effect on the hemoglobin levels of anemic patients, the current (or baseline) hemoglobin levels of 6 randomly chosen anemic patients were measured by a hospital. They were 9.2, 11.0, 10.1, 8.8, 11.5 and 9.7. Then they were instructed to take an iron pill at breakfast time every day for a month and their new hemoglobin levels were 9.9, 12.3, 10.5, 9.0, 11.6 and 10.1. At a 1% level, does the iron supplement have significant beneficial effect?
