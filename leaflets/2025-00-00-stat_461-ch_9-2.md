## Problem 2

A random sample of 5 college-age women was asked about their own heights and their mothers' heights. The researchers wanted to know whether college women are taller on an average than their mothers. The results (in inches) were 66, 64, 64, 69, 67 for the daughters and 66, 62, 65, 66, 63 for the corresponding mothers. (a) What is the population parameter difference of interest here? (b) Construct a 95% confidence interval for that parameter difference. (c) Based on this confidence interval, what can you say about the comparison between population mean height of the daughters and that for their mothers? [Hint: Although this isn't a 'before-after' comparison, there is a clear one-to-one correspondence between a woman and her mother. It is a 'matched pair' comparison]
