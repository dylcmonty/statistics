## Problem 2

A CBS News poll conducted in the US in November 16-19, 2012 asked "Are you in favor of legalizing the use of marijuana". In the nationwide poll of $n=1100$ adults, 517 said that they favored legalization. Write down a 90% confidence interval for the true, unknown population proportion of adults supporting the legalization of marijuana in the US. Next, write down a 99% confidence interval for the same parameter.
