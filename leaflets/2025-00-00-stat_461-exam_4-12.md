## Problem 12

A random sample of 39 second graders who participated in regular sports had manual dexterity scores with mean 32.19 and standard deviation 4.34. An independent sample of 37 second graders (who did not participate in regular sports) showed manual dexterity scores with mean 31.68 and standard deviation 4.56. At the 5% significance level, is there sufficient evidence to claim that second graders who participate in regular sports have a higher mean score?
