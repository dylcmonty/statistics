## Problem 5

If the random vector $(X,Y)$ has the joint pdf $f(x,y)=k(1-x)$ on $0\le y\le x\le1$ and $=0$ elsewhere, what must $k$ be for $f(x,y)$ to be a legitimate joint density? Find the marginal densities of $X$ and $Y$. Find the conditional density function of $X$, given that $Y=y$. Are $X$ and $Y$ independent?
