## 3

About 10% of the human population is left-handed. Suppose a researcher speculates that artists are more likely to be left-handed than other people in the general population. The researcher surveys 150 artists and finds that 18 of them are left-handed. (a) What’s the population parameter of interest here? (b) State the research (or alternative) hypothesis and the null hypothesis. (c) Carry out the test at a 5% significance level. What is the p-value of this test?
