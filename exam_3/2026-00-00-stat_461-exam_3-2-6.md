## 6

The proponents of a diet plan claim that the mean amount of weight lost in the first 3 weeks under the plan is more than 10 pounds. A consumer advocacy group is skeptical and measures the beginning and ending weights for a random sample of 20 people who followed the plan for 3 weeks. The sample mean and sample standard deviation of the weight lost during this period are 8 pounds and 3.5 pounds respectively. What is the population here and what is the parameter of interest? What are the research hypothesis and the null hypothesis? Compute the test statistic and carry out the test using the appropriate table. Use a significance level of 5%.
