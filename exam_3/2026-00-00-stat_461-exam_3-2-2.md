Write the research (or alternative) hypothesis and the null hypothesis for each of the following potential research questions in symbols. (a) Do college students without a student loan tend to take more credit-hours, on average, than the 18 they can take without paying extra tuition? (b) Has the average amount of daily sales at a carry-out Asian fast food outlet changed from $525 (the average amount it used to be before the COVID pandemic) after they added an outside terrace dining option during the pandemic? (c) Is the proportion of employees earning the federal minimum wage at a nationwide motel chain under a new management lower that 35% (i.e. what it used to be under its previous management)? (d) Is a coin biased?
